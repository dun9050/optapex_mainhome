Capability 01

Real-Time Bid Optimization

Adjusts bid prices and placement multipliers continuously using live performance data. Instead of fixed rules, the algorithm weighs current CVR, competition, and margin contribution to find the bid that maximizes profit — not just clicks.

For new targeting with no history, it starts with low discovery bids and applies subtle increments to gather data efficiently before committing budget.

Capability 02

Bid & Budget Dayparting

Conversion rates vary significantly by hour. Optapex identifies time windows with higher conversion potential from your historical data and automatically raises bids and allocates budget during those hours — and lowers them during low-efficiency periods.

Capability 03

Intelligent Keyword Harvesting

AI continuously scans search term reports to identify high-converting queries not yet in your campaigns. New keywords are automatically added to the correct campaigns based on your objective — exact match for converters, broad for discovery.

Capability 04

Automatic Keyword Negation

The algorithm continuously evaluates every search term for cost inefficiency. Terms that repeatedly consume budget without converting are automatically negated — stopping the bleed before it compounds over weeks of manual review cycles.

▸ Evaluating 1,284 search terms...
✕ "cheap gaming mouse" — 42 clicks, 0 orders → NEGATED
✕ "mouse for office" — $18.40 spend, ACoS 310% → NEGATED
✓ Saved: $26.80/week in wasted ad spend
