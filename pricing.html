FAQ

Questions before you commit

The answers every seller asks before starting their Optapex trial.

After 30 days, your account moves to your selected paid plan. You'll receive an email reminder before the trial ends so there are no surprises. You can cancel or change plans at any time from your dashboard.
If your monthly ad spend is approaching $10,000, we recommend starting on Pro. The larger engine count and advanced features like Budget Simulator and Brand Defense become directly ROI-positive at that scale, and the additional cost is typically offset within the first month.
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. There are no penalties for switching.
Optapex reports are AI-compiled summaries that combine campaign performance, budget flow, product priority signals, and competitive data into a single structured document. Pro users get 3 per week; Pro+ users get unlimited generation.
White Glove Onboarding means a dedicated Optapex success manager handles your account setup end-to-end — including Amazon Ads API connection, campaign structure review, goal alignment, and first-week optimization configuration. For the first 3 months, you also receive monthly performance analysis sessions.
Optapex delivers its strongest value for sellers managing multiple campaigns and SKUs simultaneously — typically 5 or more active ASINs. For a single-product operation, the Lite plan still provides meaningful automation, though the portfolio-level intelligence features will have less data to work with.
Optapex is built and operated on LG CNS enterprise infrastructure — the same platform that powers large-scale LG Group operations. This provides enterprise-grade reliability, security, and data handling that self-hosted or startup-operated tools cannot match.
