Profit Optimization

ROAS is a ratio. Profit is what you can spend tomorrow.

Most platforms optimize for ROAS because it's measurable and looks good in reports. But ROAS is a ratio — Revenue ÷ Ad Spend. It tells you nothing about your margin, cost of goods, returns, or fees.

A ROAS of 8× on a product with a 5% margin is worse than a ROAS of 4× on a product with a 40% margin. Optapex knows the difference. It factors in product-level cost structures and routes budget to where incremental ad spend returns the highest real profit.
