Portfolio Intelligence

Portfolio-Level Intelligence,
Down to the Keyword

While most platforms optimize campaign by campaign, Optapex manages your entire Optimization Set as a connected portfolio — dynamically reallocating budget and bids across ASINs, campaigns, and keywords based on where your next dollar creates the most impact.

💸

Dynamic Budget Allocation

Budgets flow toward your highest-potential ASINs and campaigns, recalculated daily based on performance, remaining budget, and time left in your optimization window.

🎯

Keyword-Level Bid Precision

Every keyword gets an individual bid based on its expected contribution to your portfolio goals. Low performers are deprioritized automatically — no wasted spend, no manual cleanup.

🔍

Cannibalization Detection

If ad spend is replacing organic sales rather than growing total volume, Optapex detects the pattern and pulls back investment. You only pay to grow.

Keyword Harvesting

Automated Keyword
Discovery and Management

Optapex continuously scouts your Auto and Phrase campaigns for high-potential search terms — and flags the ones wasting your budget. Winning keywords are promoted into Manual campaigns with optimized bids. Underperformers are suppressed automatically.

🧠

AI-Powered Classification

Keywords are evaluated across multiple signals — impressions, clicks, CTR, conversions, ROAS — not just one metric in isolation.

✂️

Positive and Negative Harvesting

Good keywords are promoted and bid-optimized. Bad keywords are soft-negated to stop budget bleed while maintaining search visibility.
